## Key Concepts

### The Five-Storey Building

```
F5: GOVERNANCE (Gold)     — Rules, protocols, decision-making
F4: COMMUNITY (Red)       — People, relationships, communication
F3: OPERATIONS (Amber)    — Workflows, processes, automation
F2: DATA (Green)          — Information layer, analytics, storage
F1: INFRASTRUCTURE (Blue) — Foundation systems, APIs, connectivity
```

### The Rooftop Society (11 Avatars)

**Inner Ring (5):**
- Project Manager (Lion)
- Situations Manager (Hawk)
- Companion (Golden Retriever)
- Source of Truth (Owl)
- "I Got a Guy" Connector (Bear)

**Special (2):**
- Angel of Your Better Nature (Phoenix, center)
- Voice of Concern (Purple Cat, right corner)

**Outer Ring & Edges (4):**
- QA Sentinel (Eagle)
- Journalist (Hummingbird)
- Swarm Workers (Bees)
- Wisdom Giants (Translucent, semi-transparent)

### Three Decision-Making Systems

**System 1 — Intuitive** (Voice of Concern)
- Fast, automatic, emotional
- Gut feelings, safety concerns

**System 2 — Rational** (Professional Team)
- Slow, deliberate, logical
- Project management, coordination

**System 3 — Moral** (Angel of Your Better Nature)
- Conscience, moral compass
- Appears during difficult decisions

### Swiss Governance Model

**Subsidiarity:** "Nothing that can be done at a lower level should be done at a higher level."

**Three Levels:**
- Commune — Individual Bingo Card (maximum local autonomy)
- Canton — Initiative Cluster / Pope's Domain (regional coordination)
- Confederation — HB1000 / Master Jeeves (system-wide decisions)

**Petition Escalation (5 Levels):**
1. Rooftop Discussion
2. Formal Petition (10+ endorsements)
3. Inner Ring Vote
4. Pope Review (cross-initiative implications)
5. Master Jeeves (benevolent dictator override)

### Ruby Red Client-Centric Design

**The Ruby Red Persona:**
- 35-45 year old mom of two
- Working poor, financially stretched
- Makes difficult daily decisions
- Unbanked/underbanked
- Needs empathetic, practical support

**Core Insight:**
> "It's expensive to be poor. It's free to have a city working for you."

**Move 37:**
> "Ruby Red does not just benefit from Bingo City. Ruby Red gets her own Bingo City."
