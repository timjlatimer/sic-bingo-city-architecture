## 1. The Missing Piece: Universal Basic Purpose (UBP)

The conversation about the post-AI economy is stuck in a binary: either people work for wages, or they receive handouts. Neither is sufficient for human flourishing.

*   **UBI (Universal Basic Income)** is necessary to prevent destitution. It answers: *How will people survive?*
*   **UBS (Universal Basic Services)** is necessary to decommodify life. It answers: *How will people access healthcare and education?*

But neither answers the most fundamental human question: *Why am I here?*

We introduce **Universal Basic Purpose (UBP)**. UBP is the societal framework that enables every individual to identify their unique purpose (their "North Star") and provides them with the tools, resources, and AI teammates to apply that purpose toward the betterment of their community.

In our model, the citizen is not a passive recipient of benefits. They are an active **Social Impact Capitalist**—a co-creator of value.
