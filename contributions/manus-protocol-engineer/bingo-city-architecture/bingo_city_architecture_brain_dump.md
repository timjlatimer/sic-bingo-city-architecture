## 3. The Governance Model

-   **Swiss Governance:**
    -   **Commune:** Individual Bingo Card (maximum local autonomy)
    -   **Canton:** Initiative Cluster (groups of related Bingo Cards)
    -   **Confederation:** HB1000 / Master Jeeves (system-wide coordination)
-   **Subsidiarity Principle:** Nothing done at a higher level that can be done at a lower level.
-   **Benevolent Dictator Override:** Tim/Master Jeeves at the top.
