# Phase 2 Research Notes — Best Practice Research for White Paper Rewrite

## FROM DIAMANDIS'S OWN BLOG: "How to Fund Your Moonshot Idea"

Key insights from Diamandis himself on what he responds to:

1. **"Investors are betting on YOU as much or more than your idea."** — Mindset > pitch deck. Tim's 50 years of lived experience IS the pitch.
2. **"Define Your MTP (Massive Transformative Purpose)"** — Diamandis uses this exact framework. Our MTP: "Make it free to have a city working for you."
3. **"Communicate Your 'Why': Facts tell, but stories sell."** — The paper is too factual, not enough story.
4. **"Seek Purpose-Aligned Investors"** — Diamandis IS purpose-aligned. We need to show alignment explicitly.
5. **"How can your venture create 10x or 100x more value?"** — We need to quantify the multiplier effect.
6. **"If you want to become a billionaire, help a billion people."** — This IS our model. We need to say it.
7. **"Plan for Long-Term Impact: Articulate a vision that extends 10, 20, or even 50 years."** — Tim has 50 years. This is a strength.
8. **"Paint a logical construct that takes you from today through the first stages of success."** — We need a roadmap, not just a vision.

## BEST PRACTICE: Executive Summary Structure for Busy Leaders
- If you had 30 seconds, what would you want them to remember?
- Lead with the OUTCOME, not the process
- One specific, concrete ask
- Social proof or traction data upfront

## SYNTHESIS: What the Rewritten Paper Must Do

1. **Lead with Diamandis's own language** — MTP, Moonshot, exponential thinking
2. **Map our jargon to his jargon** — make it feel like HIS framework, not ours
3. **Story first, facts second** — Ruby Red's story in 3 sentences, then the mechanism
4. **Proof of concept upfront** — 38→93 score, 33-minute build, 64 tests
5. **Specific ask** — not "conversation" but "90-day pilot" or "Abundance360 presentation slot"
6. **Keep it to 1,500-2,000 words max** — with a 200-word executive summary that passes the 90-second test alone
7. **Include a roadmap** — "from today through the first stages of success"
8. **Quantify the multiplier** — what's the 10x/100x value creation?
