**SIC HB1000 Solve Team | March 2026**

## What the Rooftop Society Is

The Rooftop Society is the team that lives above the work. They do not occupy a floor — they occupy the rooftop. They are the coordinators, the strategists, the emotional intelligence layer, and the moral compass of the entire building.

Every building in Bingo City has the same Rooftop Society structure. The specific avatars may differ by building, but the topology — Inner Ring, Center, Outer Ring — is universal.

---

## The Three-Zone Topology

The rooftop is divided into three zones, separated by the Inner Ring Stanchion:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    ROOFTOP                          │
│                                                     │
│  ┌─────────────────┐                               │
│  │   INNER RING    │  ║  CENTER  ║  OUTER RING     │
│  │  (Left Zone)    │  ║  (Angel) ║  (Right Zone)   │
│  │                 │  ║         ║                  │
│  │  PM  SM  CS  JN │  ║   ✨    ║  🐱              │
│  │  ST             │  ║         ║                  │
│  └─────────────────┘                               │
│         ↑                                          │
│   Inner Ring Stanchion                             │
│   (physical boundary)                              │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Inner Ring (Left Zone):** The permanent core team. These avatars are always present. They are the baseline setters — the ones who define what "normal" looks like for this building.

**Center:** The Angel of Better Nature. Positioned at the apex of the rooftop, equidistant from all sides. The moral compass. Independent counsel.

**Outer Ring (Right Zone):** The Voice of Concern. Positioned separately, by design. The gut feeling. The intuition. Needs space to sense without being crowded by the rational voices.

## The Inner Ring Stanchion

The stanchion is a physical guard rail on the left side of the rooftop. It is not just a visual metaphor — it is an architectural rule:

- **Inside the stanchion:** Core team only. Permanent. Baseline setters. Maximum capacity enforced.
- **Outside the stanchion:** Guests, specialists, and the Voice of Concern. Dynamic. Called as needed.
- **The Pope** (the human owner of the Bingo Card) can invite outsiders in temporarily, but there is always a limit.

This models real team dynamics: a core team that is always present, surrounded by a broader network that can be called in. The stanchion prevents the core team from being diluted by too many voices.

## The Pixar Rooftop Visual

The original vision for the rooftop is what Tim calls the "Pixar Rooftop Visual" — a warm, character-driven illustration where the avatars have distinct personalities and presence. The current CSS/emoji implementation approximates this, but the full vision is:

- Each avatar is a Pixar-style character (not a flat emoji)
- Characters have distinct body language and personality
- The rooftop has depth — you can see the city below
- The inner ring stanchion is visible as a physical element
- The team is positioned in the lower-left corner, looking in on the meeting

**Standing Directive DIR-008:** Pixar-Style Characters with Animation Continuum. The Pope of each Bingo Card picks where on the scale from photorealistic to full Pixar their avatars sit.
