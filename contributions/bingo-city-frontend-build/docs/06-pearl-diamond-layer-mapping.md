**SIC HB1000 Solve Team | March 2026**

## What the PEARL Diamond Is

The PEARL Diamond is the universal framework that maps how every initiative in the HB1000 ecosystem is structured. It is called a "diamond" because it has nested layers that expand outward from a central point — like a diamond when viewed from above.

PEARL stands for the five nested layers of any initiative:

| Letter | Layer | What It Is |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| **P** | Purpose | The North Star — why this initiative exists |
| **E** | Ecosystem | The people, partners, and resources around the initiative |
| **A** | Architecture | The structural design — buildings, floors, systems |
| **R** | Relationships | The human connections — Ruby Red, team, community |
| **L** | Learning | The feedback loops — how the initiative gets smarter over time |

---

## How Bingo City Maps to PEARL

### P — Purpose

**For the Ruby Red Maven city:**  
Purpose = "A team of AI helpers working around the clock to make life less expensive for Ruby Red."

This is the North Star that every building, every floor, every square serves. When a square is being built, the question is always: does this make life less expensive for Ruby Red? If not, it does not belong.

**In the UI:** The Purpose is expressed in the subtitle on the Welcome page: *"A simple 5×5 Bingo Card that carries an entire AI civilization. Your personal city of advocates, working around the clock to make life less expensive for you."*

---

### E — Ecosystem

**For the Ruby Red Maven city:**  
Ecosystem = the six buildings + the Rooftop Society + the human wisdom giants who advise the team.

The ecosystem is not just the technology. It is the people around the technology: the SIC Solve Team members who are wisdom giants in their domains, the community partners who provide resources, the government programs that Benefits Navigator navigates, the stores that Deal Hunter monitors.

**In the UI:** The ecosystem is visible in the Browse page — six buildings, each with its own team and North Star.

---

### A — Architecture

**For the Ruby Red Maven city:**  
Architecture = the five-floor system + the 25-square bingo grid + the Rooftop Society topology.

This is the structural layer — the buildings, floors, windows, and rooftop. The architecture is what makes the ecosystem visible and navigable.

**In the UI:** The architecture is the entire Bingo City front-end — the IsometricBuilding component, the BingoGrid, the RooftopTeam, the city view.

---

### R — Relationships

**For the Ruby Red Maven city:**  
Relationships = Ruby Red's relationship with her Companion, her trust in the system, her sense of being seen and heard.

The Relationships layer is where empathy lives. It is the reason the Voice of Concern exists on the Rooftop — to ensure the system never loses sight of how Ruby Red *feels*, not just what she *needs*.

**In the UI:** Relationships are expressed in the copy ("Your AI best friend. Always with you."), the avatar warmth, and the "Join the City" overlay that welcomes Ruby Red personally.

---

### L — Learning

**For the Ruby Red Maven city:**  
Learning = the V13 Learning Loop protocol applied to every initiative. The feedback loops. The Feedback Loop square (F5, position 22). The Transparency Report (F5, position 23).

The Learning layer ensures the city gets smarter over time. Every interaction Ruby Red has with the system teaches the system something. The Source of Truth avatar guards the accuracy of what the system has learned.

**In the UI:** Learning is expressed in the Governance floor (F5) — Privacy Controls, Feedback Loop, Transparency Report, Ethics Guardian, Community Standards.

## The PEARL Diamond in the Two-Level Bingo Card Framework

The PEARL Diamond applies at both levels of the Bingo Card framework:

**Prime Directive Level (above the line):**
- P = Tim's personal North Star ("Touch the hand of someone who changed the world")
- E = The SIC ecosystem — all four initiatives + the wisdom giants
- A = The HB1000 architecture — the constitutional memory system, the enforcement engine
- R = Tim's relationships — with his team, with Ruby Red, with the community
- L = The V13/V14 Learning Loop — the meta-learning system

**Local Directive Level (below the line):**
- P = Each initiative's North Star (e.g., "The Poor Man's Bank")
- E = Each initiative's ecosystem (e.g., CashCo's pawn shop network)
- A = Each initiative's Bingo City architecture
- R = Each initiative's relationship with its beneficiary
- L = Each initiative's learning loops

## Where Marketing Appears in the PEARL Diamond

This is the open question that the marketing placement research addresses. The short mapping:

| PEARL Layer | Marketing Dimension |
|-------------|-------------------|
| P (Purpose) | Brand story — why we exist, told in a way that resonates with Ruby Red |
| E (Ecosystem) | Channel strategy — where Ruby Red is, how we reach her |
| A (Architecture) | Marketing infrastructure — content systems, social media, distribution |
| R (Relationships) | Community building — peer networks, word of mouth, trust |
| L (Learning) | Analytics — what's working, what's not, how we improve |

Marketing is not a single layer in the PEARL Diamond. It is present in all five layers. This is why it should NOT be a single floor in the building — it should be distributed across all floors, with a Marketing avatar on the Rooftop to coordinate it.
