# CONV-005: The PEARL Diamond Layer Mapping

**Type:** Conversation Reconstruction  
**Context:** How the PEARL Diamond maps to the Bingo City building architecture  
**Significance:** Establishes the health measurement framework for each building

---

## What the PEARL Diamond Is

The PEARL Diamond is a five-dimension health framework for each initiative. It maps directly to the Bingo City visualization:

| Letter | Dimension | Visual Representation in Bingo City |
|--------|-----------|-------------------------------------|
| **P** | Progress | Overall building brightness (% of green windows) |
| **E** | Engagement | Rooftop Society presence (are avatars on the roof?) |
| **A** | Alignment | North Star drift indicator (subtle color shift if drifting) |
| **R** | Resilience | Ratio of amber to gray windows (in-progress vs. blocked) |
| **L** | Legacy | Building height (taller buildings = higher legacy potential) |

---

## The PEARL Diamond and the Bingo Card

The PEARL Diamond is not just a Bingo City concept — it is a universal health framework for any initiative in the HB1000. Every Bingo Card can be evaluated against the PEARL Diamond. The Bingo City visualization makes the PEARL Diamond visible at a portfolio level.

---

## The Alignment Dimension — The Most Important

The Alignment dimension (A) is the most important and the most difficult to measure. It answers the question: "Is this initiative still working toward its original North Star, or has it drifted?"

Drift is the silent killer of initiatives. An initiative can be making progress (high P), have engaged team members (high E), be resilient (high R), and have high legacy potential (high L) — but if it has drifted from its North Star, all of that progress is in the wrong direction.

The Three Guardians in the Learning Loop are specifically responsible for detecting and flagging Alignment drift. The Devil's Advocate challenges every conclusion. The North Star's Voice ensures recommendations stay aligned. The Pragmatist grounds everything in reality.

---

## The PEARL Diamond and the Learning Loop

The PEARL Diamond feeds directly into the Learning Loop. Each phase of the Learning Loop produces a PEARL Diamond score for each initiative. Over time, the Learning Loop tracks whether PEARL scores are improving or declining, and recommends interventions when scores fall below threshold.

This creates a self-correcting system: the Bingo City visualization shows the current state, the Learning Loop analyzes the trend, and the Three Guardians recommend action.
