# CONV-002: How the 6 Buildings Were Named and Assigned

**Type:** Conversation Reconstruction  
**Context:** The selection and assignment of the 6 initiatives to the 6 Bingo City buildings  
**Significance:** Establishes the canonical building roster and their identities

---

## The Six Buildings — Selection Rationale

The six buildings in Bingo City were selected to represent the six most active and architecturally distinct initiatives in the SIC HB1000 portfolio as of March 2026. They were chosen to represent a range of:

- **Progress levels** (from 25% to 88%)
- **Floor counts** (3 to 5 floors)
- **Functional domains** (capture, wisdom, community, advocacy, accountability, housing)
- **Color identities** (each building has a distinct color that reflects its personality)

---

## Building Identity Rationale

**Cloud Butterfly 🦋 (Orange, 5 floors, 72%)**
Orange was chosen because it represents warmth, energy, and the spontaneous nature of creative insight. The butterfly emoji captures the ephemeral quality of cloud butterflies — they must be caught before they fly away. Five floors because Cloud Butterfly is the most architecturally complete initiative, with the full F1-F5 stack from Foundation to Transcendence.

**Wisdom Giants 🧠 (Purple, 4 floors, 45%)**
Purple was chosen for its association with wisdom, depth, and the mystical. The brain emoji captures the intelligence dimension. Four floors because Wisdom Giants has a clear four-layer architecture (capture, fusion, delivery, community) but has not yet reached the Transcendence layer.

**Digger Cafe 🏗️ (Emerald, 3 floors, 88%)**
Emerald green was chosen for its association with growth, community, and the natural world. The construction emoji captures the "building something together" spirit. Three floors because Digger Cafe is operationally focused — it doesn't need a five-floor architecture. Its near-88% completion makes it the most "lit up" building in the city.

**Effn Duck 🦆 (Pink, 4 floors, 33%)**
Pink was chosen for its disruptive, unexpected quality — Effn Duck is the advocacy engine, the one that calls out the three gangsters. The duck emoji captures the "duck out of water" quality of speaking truth to power. Four floors because advocacy has a clear four-layer architecture (identification, advocacy, accountability, amplification). At 33%, this building is still mostly dark — there is significant work ahead.

**PTK Promises 📚 (Cyan, 3 floors, 60%)**
Cyan was chosen for its association with clarity, transparency, and the digital world. The books emoji captures the "keeping your word" dimension. Three floors because PTK has a clean three-layer architecture (foundation, intelligence, ecosystem integration).

**Seba Hub 🏠 (Amber, 3 floors, 25%)**
Amber was chosen for its warm, homey quality — Seba Hub is about housing stability and community. The house emoji is the most direct possible representation. At 25%, Seba Hub is the least developed building in the city — mostly dark windows, with significant work ahead.

---

## The Color Palette Decision

The six building colors were chosen to be:
1. **Visually distinct** — no two buildings look similar at a glance
2. **Emotionally resonant** — each color reflects the initiative's personality
3. **Dark-theme compatible** — all colors glow well against the dark background (#0f172a)
4. **Accessible** — sufficient contrast for Ruby Red Maven on a mobile screen in various lighting conditions

The dark theme (#0f172a background) was chosen specifically because it makes the glowing windows more dramatic and legible — like a real city at night.
