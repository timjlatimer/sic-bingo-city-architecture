# CONV-006: The Original Marketing Question That Triggered This Brain Dump

**Type:** Conversation Reconstruction  
**Context:** The question from Tim that triggered the creation of this entire repo  
**Significance:** Establishes the research question that the `/research/` directory answers

---

## The Question

Tim asked, in the context of the Bingo City rebuild and the Master Jeeves brain dump request:

> **"Where should Marketing live across the entire HB1000 architecture?"**

This was not a simple question. It had multiple dimensions:

1. **Above the HB1000 line** — marketing the moonshot/SIC brand
2. **Below the line** — marketing individual initiatives
3. **Inside Bingo City** — own floor? half of Operations? new F6?
4. **Rooftop Society** — marketing avatar on inner ring?
5. **Sub-agents** — content, social, growth, brand, community
6. **PEARL Diamond placement** — which dimension does marketing affect most?
7. **Floor naming** — should each floor have a named identity?

---

## Why This Question Is Hard

Marketing is a cross-cutting concern. Unlike Cloud Butterfly (which is clearly a capture system) or PTK Promises (which is clearly an accountability system), Marketing touches every initiative simultaneously. It is:

- **Above the line** when it's about the SIC brand and the moonshot
- **Below the line** when it's about driving Ruby Red to Maven
- **Inside each building** when it's about communicating the initiative's progress
- **On the rooftop** when it's about the people doing the marketing work

The question of where Marketing "lives" is really the question of whether Marketing is a building, a floor, a rooftop position, a sub-agent, or all of the above.

Tim's question came in the context of the Bingo City rebuild — a moment when the architecture was being reconsidered from the ground up. This was the right moment to answer the marketing placement question, because the answer will affect:
