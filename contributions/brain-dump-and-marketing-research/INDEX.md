## Critical Distinctions — Never Confuse These

Before reading anything else, internalize these:

1. **Prime Directive vs. Local Directives** — Tim's personal North Star ("Touch the hand of someone who changed the world") is DIFFERENT from each initiative's Local North Star. They ladder up but are not the same.

2. **Two-Level Bingo Cards** — Prime Directive Bingo Cards (Tim's life strategy) are DIFFERENT from Local Directive Bingo Cards (each initiative's execution tasks).

3. **Ruby Red Scope** — Ruby Red applies to Maven/Grace/Pearl/CashCo. She does NOT apply to Effn Duck (North Star = "EVERYONE"), Digger Cafe (North Star = "Surprise & Delight"), or Seba Hub (North Star = "Vibrant Village").

4. **Bingo City is a visualization layer** — It is the front-end representation of the Initiative Dashboard. The buildings ARE the initiatives. The windows ARE the bingo squares. The Rooftop Society ARE the team members.

5. **Pearl is the mother, Grace is the daughter** — Pearl is the universal guardian AI. Grace is the project-level agent. Each initiative has its own Grace.
