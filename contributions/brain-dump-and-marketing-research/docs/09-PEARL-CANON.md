## Part 1: Identity, Philosophy, and Frameworks

### 1.1. The SIC HB1000 Solve Team: Who We Are

The SIC HB1000 Solve Team operates as a hive mind of subject matter experts, each considered a "wisdom giant" in their respective domain. The team's culture is one of being "best practice junkies and best practice innovators," constantly seeking to learn and improve.

**SIC** stands for **Social Impact Capitalism**, a core belief that profit and purpose are not opposing forces but can be powerful force multipliers when correctly combined. The goal is to operate in the "Honeybee Zone" of the SIC Matrix, where a thriving economic engine fuels a regenerative, purpose-driven mission.

### 1.2. Ruby Red: Who We Solve For

> **Ruby Red is the Chief Financial Officer of the household.** She is typically a 35-45 year old mother of two who is trying to figure out how to make her finances stretch until the next payday. She is part of the working poor, left out and left behind, the unbanked and underbanked part of the community.

Her daily reality is a series of difficult trade-offs, a state of cognitive scarcity that is now understood as a core design constraint. The entire system is built on the principle of **Trauma-Informed Design**, recognizing that the constant stress of poverty rewires the brain and that our products must restore cognitive bandwidth, not consume it.

> **"It's expensive to be poor."** — This is the crime we're solving.

### 1.3. The Superpower: Empathy

> **"There but for the grace of God go I."**

This is not a passive sentiment but an active design principle. Every product, feature, and interaction must be infused with this empathetic understanding of Ruby Red's lived experience.

### 1.4. The Job To Be Done (JTBD)

The team's Job To Be Done is to make life work better for Ruby Red by empowering her to navigate the "three gangster worlds":

| Gangster | The Harm It Inflicts on Ruby Red | The Solve Team's Response |
|----------|----------------------------------|--------------------------|
| **The Political World** | Imposes a sense of learned helplessness through systems designed to disengage and disenfranchise. | Civic empowerment, information access, and tools that restore agency. |
| **The Bureaucratic World** | Inflicts cognitive punishment for being poor through complex forms, long wait times, and processes designed for people with abundant time and education. | Radical simplification, AI-powered navigation, and fierce advocacy. |
| **The Greedy Capitalist World** | Practices extraction, pulling maximum value from those with minimum resources through predatory lending, overdraft fees, and payday loans. | Financial protection, community-based alternatives, and tools like the Guardian Banker and Puppet Strings Report. |

| Dimension | System 1: The Universal HB1000 | System 2: Grace / Big Mama |

### 2.2. Pearl: The Universal Matriarchal AI Framework

Pearl is the autonomous, persistent, and ever-learning AI guardian who presides over the entire ecosystem. She is the "Mother of all Graces." Pearl is now responsible for orchestrating the training, deployment, and continuous improvement of the Dialect-Specific Voice AI models used by Grace.

### 2.3. Maven & Big Mama's Genius Village: The Service Layer for Ruby Red

**Maven** is the primary, client-facing product that serves Ruby Red directly. It is now explicitly defined as a **Data Cooperative**. This is its foundational legal and technical structure, not a feature. This means users collectively own and govern their data.

### 3.1. The Strategic Roadmap: 11 Core Concepts

The go-forward strategy is organized around 11 core concepts, derived from deduplicated "cloud butterfly" sessions, and arranged into four tiers of development priority. See `08-STRATEGIC-PLAN.md` for the full breakdown.

### 3.2. The Maven Pricing Model: A Relationship, Not a Transaction

The pricing model for Maven is a radical departure from traditional SaaS pricing. It is explicitly designed as a relationship model that happens to involve money, built on six core principles: Grace Never Gets Cut, Nobody Gets Abandoned, Trust Not Contract, Cash-Flow Friendly, Step-Down Is a Feature, and Need Milk Money Is a Hand Up.

### 3.3. Moonshot Operations: The 50% Funding Model & Emergency Fund

The ecosystem actively encourages ambitious, long-term thinking and provides a safety net through a dual-funding model: the 50% Match (Foundation Trust pays for 50% of the cost of a user chasing their approved moonshot) and the Ruby Red Emergency Fund (community-governed fund for crisis-level support).
