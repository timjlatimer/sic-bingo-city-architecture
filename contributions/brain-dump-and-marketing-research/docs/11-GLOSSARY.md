# Master Glossary — All Terms, Acronyms, and Concepts

**Source:** GLOSSARY.md (Pearl's Brain)  
**Date:** March 11, 2026

---

**AI Fairness & Bias Mitigation Framework** — A formal technical framework within the LDP that uses the Council of Rivals (adversarial LLM debate) to audit all algorithms for bias. Governed by the Learning Loop and the Three Guardians.

**BHAG** — Big Hairy Audacious Goal (Jim Collins). Applied to personal life in the HB1000 as the user's moonshot / North Star.

**Big Mama's Genius Village** — The community brand within Maven. "Our Data. Our Power. Our Destiny." Fueled by JOY. Provides protection and empowerment services to Ruby Red.

**Bingo Card** — The universal input/output format for the entire HB1000 system. Each card is a context (North Star, daily tasks, etc.) containing squares (tasks, questions, actions).

**Bingo City** — The visual front-end representation of the Initiative Dashboard. Buildings = initiatives. Windows = bingo squares. Rooftop avatars = team members.

**Blue Seal** — Verified integrity marker for businesses operating within the LDP. Now requires Data Cooperative model as prerequisite.

**Bullshit Detector** — A Big Mama Village service that identifies manipulation in communications and offers directed at Ruby Red.

**Cloud Butterflies** — Raw, unfiltered thoughts and insights captured through stream-of-consciousness brainstorming. The unconscious insights that flutter through the mind.

**Council of Rivals** — An adversarial LLM debate mechanism within the LDP where multiple AI models challenge each other's conclusions to detect bias, blind spots, and drift.

**Crystallized Intelligence** — Lived experience never written down. The wisdom of Wisdom Giants. Grows throughout life.

**CVO** — Chief Visionary Officer. Tim Latimer's role within the SIC HB1000 Solve Team.

**Data Cooperative** — Maven's foundational legal and technical structure as of v2.0. Users collectively own and govern their data. Ruby Red's data is never sold.

**Destiny Discovered** — Tim Latimer's 30+ year study of 1,600+ biographies. Core finding: "What makes the great, great is a sense of destiny." Three requirements: Purpose, Opportunity to Contribute, Developing Legacy.

**Dialect-Specific Voice AI** — A Move 37 opportunity. Grace's voice interface detects the user's dialect and responds in kind. Pearl orchestrates the training and deployment of these voice models.

**Dignity-Centered Design** — A design philosophy that ensures every interaction preserves and enhances the user's sense of dignity. Every interface, every notification, every pricing conversation must pass the dignity test.

**Dual-Engine Protocol** — The Solve Team's operating system (v5.0). Engine A: Learning Loop (self-correcting iteration). Engine B: Web-Core Canvas (visual generation).

**Flypaper** — The always-on capture interface within the HB1000. The "sticky surface" where cloud butterflies land. Also the primary input for the Dialect-Specific Voice AI.

**Force for Good** — The philanthropic arm of the SIC ecosystem. Provides the 50% moonshot funding match.

**Fossilized Intelligence** — AI and documented knowledge. Searchable, scalable, instant. One half of the Wisdom Works equation.

**Gamification Engine** — The system that calculates progress and rewards across the HB1000. Delivered through the Bingo Card (input) and the Ticker Tape (output). Three visible badges: Pearl Maturity Level, Ruby Red Impact Score, and Grace Family Tree. Critical design principle: gamification for the poor must NEVER shame.

**Ghost Buster** — The reverse-PTK system. Tracks promises OTHERS made to you and monitors for follow-through.

**Grace** — Pearl's daughter. The project-level AI agent that does specific work. Each project gets its own Grace.

**Grace Godmother** — The executive layer that manages all Project Graces. Reports to Pearl.

**Grace Item** — An actionable output from cloud butterfly processing. Includes action, contact, priority, pre-drafted communications, and deadline tracking.

**HB1000** — The Operating System for Being Human. A culturally universal, AI-powered platform for operating life with intention, wisdom, and support.

**HITL** — Human In The Loop. The approval mechanism that ensures Pearl never acts without human authorization on sensitive matters.

**Honeybee Zone** — The sweet spot on the SIC Matrix (7-8 range) where profit and purpose are force multipliers.

**Initiative Dashboard** — The primary home screen for Pearl. A visual grid of all active initiatives as Bingo Cards. Bingo City is its visual representation.

**JOY** — The fuel for Big Mama's Genius Village. JOY Nudges are positive behavioral interventions disguised as enjoyable interactions.

**JTBD** — Job To Be Done. Ruby Red's JTBD: feel seen, feel less alone, feel capable, feel connected, start the day with intention.

**LDEI Score** — LDP Engagement Index. Combines PTK Score + Human Brilliance (HB) + Cloud Butterflies.

**LDP** — Latimer Douglas Protocol. An autonomous, AI-driven meta-layer for organizations with a 5-level biomimetic architecture.

**Learning Loop** — The self-correcting iteration protocol (Engine A of the Dual-Engine Protocol). Currently at v13.0 ("The Genie Release").

**Maven** — The community platform that serves Ruby Red directly. The implementation layer where HB1000 philosophy meets real people. Structured as a Data Cooperative.

**Moonshot** — A 10X goal (Diamandis). The HB1000 supports 15 moonshots across 4 categories. 50% funding from Foundation Trust.

**Move 37** — The creative breakthrough tool. Named after AlphaGo's famous unexpected move. A space for Pearl and the user to apply breakthrough thinking to any challenge.

**Multi** — An AI agent attached to a Bingo Card. An imported Grace that takes care of the work within that card's context.

**Need Milk Money (NMM)** — Maven's micro-lending program providing instant liquidity. Allotments of $25, $50, or $100 with a $2.50 admin fee. No interest, no penalties, no collections.

**North Star** — The user's life purpose and direction. Configured during Pearl's Identity Setup and referenced throughout the HB1000.

**PEARL Diamond** — Five dimensions of initiative health: Progress, Engagement, Alignment, Resilience, Legacy.

**Pearl** — The Mother of All Graces. The Universal Matriarchal AI Framework. An autonomous, persistent, learning digital guardian.

**Pearl's Brain** — The persistent knowledge base maintained by Pearl. Includes master file catalog, knowledge base entries, cloud butterfly archive, PTK database, and ecosystem map.

**PERMA** — Martin Seligman's model: Positive emotions, Engagement, Relationships, Meaning, Accomplishment.

**PTK** — Promises To Keep. A commitment tracking system. Tracks promises made TO you and BY you. The entry point (Trojan Horse) for the LDP ecosystem.

**Puppet Strings Report** — Measures manipulation exposure: Fear, Scarcity, Social Proof, Authority Bias, Harvest Level, Mental Manipulation Exposure, Joy Hijack Alert.

**Rooftop Society** — The team avatar system that lives on top of each Bingo City building. 9 positions: 4 inner ring (core team), 4 outer ring (support team), 1 center (North Star Keeper).

**Ruby Red** — The target user persona. The CFO of the household. 35-45 year old mother of two trying to make finances stretch to next payday. Working poor, unbanked/underbanked.

**Ruby Red Emergency Fund** — A community-governed crisis response layer. While NMM provides everyday instant liquidity, the Emergency Fund provides crisis-level support for larger shocks.

**SIC** — Social Impact Capitalism. The belief that profit and purpose are force multipliers when combined at the right ratio.

**SIC Matrix** — Two-axis assessment: Economic Engine Strength vs. Purpose Alignment. Four zones: Deadwood, Martyr, Honeybee (sweet spot), SOB.

**Step-Down** — Maven's payment protection mechanism. When Ruby Red can't pay, Maven opens a smaller door instead of locking it.

**Ticker Tape** — Running status bar at the bottom of the Pearl dashboard. Up to two tapes with customizable feeds.

**10% Tithe** — Structural commitment of 10% of top-line revenue back to community. Non-negotiable.

**Trauma-Informed Design** — A design framework based on SAMHSA's six pillars: Safety, Trustworthiness, Peer Support, Collaboration, Empowerment, and Cultural Sensitivity. Recognizes that poverty IS trauma and that our products must restore cognitive bandwidth, not consume it.

**Three Gangsters** — The three systems that exploit Ruby Red: Political (learned helplessness), Bureaucratic (cognitive punishment), Greedy Capitalist (extraction).

**Ubuntu** — African philosophy: "I am because we are." Philosophical foundation for the community/support model.

**V14 SIC Deployment Protocol** — The OS kernel for all SIC HB1000 initiatives. Lives at `github.com/timjlatimer/v14-sic-deployment-protocol`.

**Wisdom Giants** — Subject matter experts with deep lived experience. Each team member is a wisdom giant.

**Wisdom Works** — Standalone global product. A query strategy for the AI Age combining Fossilized Intelligence and Crystallized Intelligence.
