## Core Principle

Find the baseline structures, not rabbit-hole nuances. Low friction, highly recognizable, Trojan Horse quality. The MVC is the smallest possible city that still feels like a city.

## The Three MVC Avatars

### 1. The Companion (Golden Retriever)
The friendly face. Talks to Ruby Red. Learns her preferences. Makes the system feel human. This is the Trojan Horse avatar — the one that gets her to come back.

### 2. The Situations Manager (Hawk)
The intelligence. Knows where Ruby Red is. Knows what she needs. Knows what is about to happen. Makes the system useful from day one.

### 3. The Voice of Concern (Cat)
The emotional anchor. Validates Ruby Red's anxiety. Honors her gut feelings. Ensures the system never optimizes away her humanity. Makes the system trustworthy.

**Why three?** Three is the minimum number that creates a **society** rather than a tool.

## Entry Domain: Grocery Budget

The MVC starts with grocery budget tracking because that is where Ruby Red lives every day. She is in the grocery store lineup making decisions about what to put back. This is the most immediate, most frequent, most emotionally charged financial decision she makes. If the system can help her here — in the moment, with empathy — she will trust it with everything else.
